Representational Works

These works are based on things I've seen or imagined and  I've been inspired to produce a tiffany-style stained glass window interpretation.  As an example, the thumbnail to the left leads to a picture of a painting by Peterson Thomas and to the right is a thumbnail of my stained glass interpretation of the painting.  Below you will see other representational works that I've created.

Some are personal to the receiver of the art, such as the Car window that I created for a Mr. Carr.  The Dove window was commissioned as an anniversary gift for parents of five.  The five roses represent their children and just about every other detail in the work relates to something in their lives.

   
  Flower Windows

My Flower Windows are taken directly from my love for nature and the inspiration that it gives me.  Some are commissioned and some speculative, but all are a true display of exactly how beautiful tiffany-style stained glass can be.
