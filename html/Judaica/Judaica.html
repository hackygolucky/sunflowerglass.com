Wedding Glass Works

Each of the following pieces are one-of-a-kind works created from the broken glasses from a couple's wedding.  As each piece of glass is different, they determine where they go on the work, I am just the willing recipient of their direction.  I can usually finish these works by the couple's first anniversary and gives them a beautiful decorative remembrance of their wedding day.
