Arch Windows

 

     Arch windows can be hung or installed, depending on the specifications of our client.  The palette used can range in both colors and textures to create a welcoming focal point to any space.

     Installation ideas could include inset into a door, sidelights for an entranceway, room separator, skylights, hung on a wall or in front of a window or as a window itself.  

     As with any custom work, a design fee will be included.  When contacting me, please include exact measurements if you plan to use this as an installation.  I will also need to know if you have certain colors that you want included in the palette along with where you will place our work and any background information that will help me to best design a window or windows that are best suited to who you are.
